Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TypeMaster Pro and typing speed tests

What is a good typing speed in WPM?

An average person types 40–50 WPM. A good professional speed is 60–70 WPM. Exceptional typists reach 80–100+ WPM. TypeMaster Pro rates you as: Beginner (0–29), Intermediate (30–49), Advanced (50–79), and Expert (80+).

Is TypeMaster Pro completely free?

Yes, 100% free! Take unlimited typing tests, view the global leaderboard, and download certificates at no cost. You don't even need an account — just start typing right away.

How is WPM (Words Per Minute) calculated?

WPM is calculated by dividing correctly typed characters by 5 (average word length), then dividing by minutes elapsed. Only correct keystrokes count — errors do not contribute to your WPM. TypeMaster Pro updates this in real time as you type.

How can I improve my typing speed quickly?

The fastest way: (1) Practice 10–15 minutes daily, (2) Focus on accuracy before speed, (3) Use all 10 fingers — touch typing, (4) Never look at the keyboard, (5) Gradually increase difficulty. Most users see measurable improvement within 2 weeks!

Does TypeMaster Pro work on mobile and tablets?

Yes! TypeMaster Pro is fully responsive and works beautifully on all devices — smartphones, tablets, and desktops. The layout automatically adjusts to any screen size. For best results on mobile, connect a Bluetooth keyboard.

How does the downloadable certificate work?

After completing a typing test, TypeMaster Pro generates a personalized Certificate of Achievement showing your name, WPM, accuracy, performance level, and date. Download it as a PNG image and share it on LinkedIn, WhatsApp, or print it out!

What are the 4 difficulty levels?

Easy: Simple everyday sentences. Medium: Technology and web-related text. Hard: Technical programming terminology. Expert: Advanced developer concepts. Each level challenges your vocabulary and accuracy differently.

Do I need to sign up to use the leaderboard?

You need a free account to appear on the global leaderboard and save results across devices. Without an account, results are saved locally in your browser. Signing up takes under 30 seconds with just an email and password!
